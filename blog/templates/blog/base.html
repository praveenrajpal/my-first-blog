VEDAS, The Infinite Knowledge

Vedas are treasure trove of knowledge of Hindus. They are not written by humans. Billions of years ago, after deep penance, sages were able to hear the voice of God which they called Veda. The sages gave that knowledge to their desciples. In this way, Vedas descended from generations to generations and still guiding the human beings regarding the correct path of living. Vedas tell us to lead a spiritual life that is filled with peace, non-violence and helping others. Vedas are divided into 4 types: Rig Veda, Yajur Veda, Sama Veda and Atharvana Veda. Vedic knowledge is also spread into smritis, upanishads and puraanas.
